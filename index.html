About Wave Cafe

Wave Coffee is a coffee shop with a bakery that takes its inspiration from a shared passion for coffee.

But not everything will be black gold. We also love, of course, to eat. At Wave Coffee we go from the sweetest to the saltiest.

How we began

We consider 2020 as our opening year, the journey that has taken us here began some time ago, merging knowledge and passion to offer the best of our experience: the trade learned and perfected thanks to various trips, courses, people, books, but above all, with the work of each day.

We are passionate about coffee, we love it, and we are happy to share it with you.
